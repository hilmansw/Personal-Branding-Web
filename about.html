About Myself

He is experienced in UI/UX Design. He skilled in Prototyping, Interface Design, Requirement Analysis, and Product Design. He successfully completed his thesis on UI/UX Design with excellent grades and got a Bachelor Degree of Computer Science from Telkom Institute of Technology Purwokerto. He is very professional in designing UI/UX both mobile and web based.

Besides that, he has an experience in teaching many students at SMK Telkom Purwokerto as an Information Technology teacher for Computer and Network Engineering and Software Engineering scopes. He has also received The Most Achievement Producer Teacher award at SMK Telkom Purwokerto and won several championships at the Banyumas district level and national level in UI/UX Design and scientific work competition at national level.

Currently, he is continuing his studies in Master Program of Information System at Diponegoro University. He has a research focus on Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Social Media Analysis in his research at the Master Degree level, because he likes learning new things.
